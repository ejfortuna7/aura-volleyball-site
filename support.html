Common questions

  • How do I follow a team? Search the team directory, open a team, and tap the star. Its schedule and rankings then appear automatically on your dashboard.
  • Where do schedules come from? Aura pulls publicly available tournament schedules from Advanced Event Systems (AES/SportsEngine) and the OVR regional calendar. You can also import practice and club calendars via iCal, Google Calendar, or your device calendar.
  • Do I need an account? No. There is no sign-in, and the app collects no personal data.
