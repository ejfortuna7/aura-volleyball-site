Aura Volleyball Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026

Aura Volleyball does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • The app does not require an account and has no sign-in.
  • Teams you favorite and events you import are stored only on your device.
  • If you grant calendar access, it is used solely to import events you choose into the app; calendar data never leaves your device.
  • The app retrieves publicly available schedules and rankings from Advanced Event Systems (advancedeventsystems.com), OVR (ovr.org), and VolleyLens (volleylens.com). Requests to these services are standard web requests and are subject to their privacy policies.
  • The app contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.
